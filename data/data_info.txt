training_images.txt: 5000 training digits made of ASCII characters, around 500 samples for each digit. Each digit is 28x28.

training_labels.txt: the correct labels for every digit from trainingimages.txt.

test_images.txt: 1000 digits to use to test the accuracy of the model generated from the training images

test_labels.txt: the correct labels for every digit from testimages.txt

training_model.txt: stores the trained model
